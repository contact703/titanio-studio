Hospital Municipal Dr. Ernesto Che Guevara

The Dr. Ernesto Che Guevara Municipal Hospital is Maricá’s main public healthcare facility, named in tribute to the revolutionary physician. It includes emergency wings, inpatient wards, corridors, surgical rooms, administrative areas, and parking facilities.
